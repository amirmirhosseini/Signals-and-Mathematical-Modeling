# Signals and Systems - Computer Assignment 5

This repository contains the MATLAB implementation and project report for the fifth computer assignment of the Signals and Systems course at the University of Tehran, focusing on Fourier analysis and frequency-based digital communication.

## Project Overview
* **Part 1 (Fourier Analysis Basics):** Analyzing discrete-time signals using Fast Fourier Transform (`fft` and `fftshift`), evaluating frequency resolution, and observing the effects of signal length, sampling rates, and phase shifts on the magnitude spectrum.
* **Part 2 (Frequency Coding & Decoding):** Implementing transmitter and receiver models to modulate text messages into continuous-time sinusoidal signals, where characters are mapped to specific discrete frequencies at different transmission speeds (1 and 5 bits/second).
* **Noise Robustness & Bandwidth Trade-off:** Simulating message decoding over a noisy Gaussian channel to determine the breakdown noise variance for each bit-rate. The analysis evaluates how increasing frequency separation (bandwidth) and adjusting the sampling rate affect signal tracking performance and overall noise immunity.
