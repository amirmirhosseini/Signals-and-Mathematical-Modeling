# Signals and Systems - Computer Assignment 6

This repository contains the MATLAB implementation and project report for the sixth computer assignment of the Signals and Systems course at the University of Tehran, focusing on radar signal processing and audio frequency analysis.

## Project Overview
* **Part 1 (Radar Distance & Speed Estimation):** Simulating a radar system to estimate target distance and velocity using time-delay ($\tau$) and Doppler frequency shifts ($f_d$) under multi-target and noisy scenarios.
* **Part 2 (Audio Processing & Music Generation):** Custom implementation of a piano note generator (`piano_note`) and musical sequence reconstruction to synthesize and play standard melodies.
* **Exercise 2-3 (Automated Audio Decoding - Incomplete):** An algorithmic approach to process a generated audio signal, isolate individual notes by detecting boundaries, and accumulate their total play durations using Fast Fourier Transform ($FFT$). While the note-splitting and frequency-matching logic are correctly structured, the temporal accumulation loop remains incomplete due to synchronization indexing issues.
